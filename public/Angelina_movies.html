The Best Movies According to Angelina

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Stardust


Great comic fantasy set in the magical kingdom of Stormhold,
where a young man searches for a fallen star for his beloved.

Spirited Away


Spirited Away is a beautiful fantasy movie about a young girl
who enters a secret world populated by strange spirits, creatures,
and sorcery, making it a quintessential example of the fantasy genre.

The Boy, the Mole, the Fox and the Horse

The Boy, the Mole, the Fox and the Horse" is categorized as a fantasy film
that tells the story of a lonely boy who befriends a kind mole, a wary fox,
and a gentle horse as they journey through a vast landscape
